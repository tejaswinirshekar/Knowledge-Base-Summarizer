Standard Operating Procedure for Waste Management

1. Waste Collection  
All waste must be collected daily and sorted into recyclable and non-recyclable materials.

2. Waste Storage  
Waste should be stored in sealed containers to prevent leakage.

3. Disposal  
Recyclable materials must be sent to certified recycling centers.  
Non-recyclable waste must be treated according to local regulations.

4. Emergency Procedures  
In case of spills, notify the safety officer immediately and follow containment protocols.
